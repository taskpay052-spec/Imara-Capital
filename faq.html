Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about borrowing with Imara Capital. Find answers to common questions below.

General Questions

Is Imara Capital licensed?

Yes! Imara Capital is a fully licensed Credit Provider under the Central Bank of Kenya (CBK). We are also registered with the Office of the Data Protection Commissioner (ODPC) and comply with all Kenyan lending regulations. Our license number is [TBD] and can be verified on the CBK website.

Do you operate in all counties?

Yes! We serve customers across all 47 counties in Kenya. As long as you have a valid National ID and an active M-Pesa account, you can apply from anywhere in the country.

What is Imara Capital's mission?

Our mission is to provide Kenyans with fair, transparent, and accessible financing. We believe in Strong Finance. Smart Growth. — helping you build a better financial future without hidden fees or predatory practices.

Application Process

What do I need to apply for a loan?

You'll need:

  • Valid Kenyan National ID
  • Active M-Pesa account (registered in your name)
  • Last 3 months' bank statement OR payslip
  • Active phone number
  • For business loans: Business registration documents
  • For asset finance: Proforma invoice
How long does the application take?

The online application form takes just 2-3 minutes to complete. Most applicants receive a decision within 24 hours, and funds are disbursed within 1 hour of acceptance.

Does checking my rate affect my credit score?

No! We use a soft pull to check your rate. This does NOT affect your credit score. Only when you accept the loan offer do we perform a full credit check.

Can I save my application and continue later?

Yes! Our application form has a Save & Resume feature. You can start your application on your phone, save it, and finish on your laptop later. We'll send you a link via SMS or email.

Rates & Fees

What are your interest rates?

Our rates vary based on your credit profile, loan amount, and term:

  • Personal Loans: 3% – 12% per month
  • Business Loans: 4% – 10% per month
  • Asset Finance: 3.5% – 8% per month

Better credit scores qualify for lower rates. Use our loan calculator to estimate your payment.

Are there any hidden fees?

Absolutely not! We believe in complete transparency. The only fees are:

  • Origination Fee: 1% – 5% (deducted at disbursement)
  • Late Payment Fee: KSh 500 or 5% of overdue amount (after 7 days)

No prepayment penalty. No early settlement fees. No hidden charges.

Can I pay my loan early?

Yes! There is no prepayment penalty. You can pay off your loan early and save on interest. Simply contact us or pay the remaining balance via M-Pesa PayBill.

Repayment

How do I make repayments?

Repayment is simple via M-Pesa PayBill:

  1. Go to M-Pesa menu
  2. Select Lipa na M-Pesa → PayBill
  3. Enter Business Number: [TBD]
  4. Enter Account Number: Your Loan ID
  5. Enter amount and PIN
  6. Confirm payment

You'll receive SMS reminders 3 days before each due date.

What happens if I miss a payment?

If you miss a payment:

  • After 7 days: A late fee of KSh 500 or 5% (whichever is higher) applies
  • After 30 days: We'll contact you to arrange a repayment plan
  • After 90 days: Default may result in CRB listing and potential legal action

We're here to help! If you're struggling, contact us early to discuss flexible repayment options.

Do you report to Credit Reference Bureaus (CRB)?

Yes, we report to licensed CRBs (Metropol and TransUnion).

Good news: Timely repayments help build your credit score! We'll notify you before any negative listing and give you an opportunity to resolve any issues.

Security & Privacy

Is my data safe with Imara Capital?

Absolutely! We are registered with the Office of the Data Protection Commissioner (ODPC) and fully comply with the Data Protection Act 2019. Your data is:

  • Encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Never shared without your consent
  • Stored on secure, ISO-certified servers
  • Only accessed by authorized personnel
Do you share my information with third parties?

We only share your information:

  • With Credit Reference Bureaus (as required by law)
  • With your explicit consent
  • When required by regulatory authorities (CBK, ODPC)

We never sell your data to marketers or third parties.

How do I request my data or delete my account?

You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of your data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to legal retention requirements)

Contact our Data Protection Officer at dpo@imaracapital.co.ke or call 0700 000 000.
