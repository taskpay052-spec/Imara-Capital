About Imara Capital

We're on a mission to provide Kenyans with fair, transparent, and accessible financing. Strong Finance. Smart Growth.

Our Story

Imara Capital was founded with a simple belief: Kenyans deserve better lending. No hidden fees. No predatory practices. Just honest, straightforward financing that helps you grow.

We saw too many Kenyans trapped in cycles of debt with digital lenders who cared more about profits than people. Hidden charges, aggressive collection tactics, and unfair terms became the norm.

We decided to do things differently. Imara Capital — meaning "strong" in Swahili — was born to be a pillar of trust in the Kenyan lending market. We're a Kenyan company, licensed by the Central Bank of Kenya, and committed to serving our community with integrity.

Today, we've helped over 5,000 Kenyans access fair financing to grow their businesses, pay school fees, handle emergencies, and build better futures.
