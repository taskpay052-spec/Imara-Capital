What You'll Need

Gather these documents before you start your application.

Personal Requirements

  • Kenyan National ID (valid)
  • Active M-Pesa account (registered in your name)
  • Phone number registered with M-Pesa
  • 18 years or older
  • Valid email address

Documents Required

  • National ID (front & back) - Photo or scan
  • Last 3 months' bank statement OR payslip
  • M-Pesa statement (recommended)
  • Business registration (for business loans)
  • Proforma invoice (for asset finance)

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers.

What credit score do I need to qualify?

We consider all credit profiles. While a good credit score helps you get better rates, we work with Kenyans at all stages of their credit journey. We use a soft pull that does not affect your credit score when you check your rate.

How fast can I get the money?

Most approved applicants receive funds within 24 hours of applying. Once you accept your offer, funds are sent to your M-Pesa within 1 hour.

Is there a penalty for paying early?

No! There is absolutely no prepayment penalty. You can pay off your loan early and save on interest. We encourage responsible borrowing and early repayment.

How do I make repayments?

Repayment is simple via M-Pesa PayBill. We'll provide you with a Business Number and your Loan ID as the account number. You'll receive monthly reminders via SMS.

Do you report to Credit Reference Bureaus (CRB)?

Yes, we report to licensed CRBs (Metropol, TransUnion). Timely repayments help build your credit score. We will notify you before any negative listing and give you an opportunity to resolve any issues.
