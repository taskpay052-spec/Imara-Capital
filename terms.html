📢 Important: These Terms constitute a legally binding agreement between you ("Borrower," "Customer," "You") and Imara Capital ("Company," "We," "Us," "Our").

2. Eligibility

To be eligible for a loan with Imara Capital, you must:

  • Be a citizen or legal resident of Kenya
  • Be at least 18 years of age
  • Have a valid Kenyan National ID
  • Have an active M-Pesa account registered in your name
  • Have a valid phone number and email address
  • Have a source of income (employment or business)
  • Not be an undischarged bankrupt
  • Not have an active default with another lender that would make repayment impossible

3. Loan Application

When you submit a loan application:

  • You confirm that all information provided is true, accurate, and complete
  • You authorize us to verify your information with third parties, including employers, banks, and Credit Reference Bureaus
  • You consent to a credit check (soft pull initially, hard pull upon acceptance)
  • You agree that providing false information may result in immediate rejection, legal action, and potential criminal liability

4. Loan Agreement

If your loan application is approved, you will enter into a Loan Agreement with Imara Capital. The Loan Agreement will specify:

  • Principal loan amount
  • Interest rate (monthly and effective APR)
  • Origination fees (if applicable)
  • Repayment schedule and due dates
  • Total repayment amount
  • Late payment fees
  • Loan term

You must review and accept the Loan Agreement before funds are disbursed. By accepting, you agree to all terms outlined in the agreement.

5. Interest & Fees

We charge interest and fees as disclosed in your Loan Agreement:

5.1 Interest Rates

  • Interest rates range from 3% to 12% per month depending on your credit profile, loan amount, and term
  • The effective Annual Percentage Rate (APR) ranges from 36% to 144%
  • Interest accrues daily on the outstanding principal balance

5.2 Fees

  • Origination Fee: 1% – 5% of loan amount (deducted at disbursement)
  • Late Payment Fee: KSh 500 or 5% of overdue amount (whichever is higher) after 7 days overdue
  • No Prepayment Penalty: You may repay early at no additional cost

⚠️ Important: Failure to repay your loan may result in additional fees, CRB listing, and legal action. Borrow responsibly.

6. Repayment Terms

Repayments must be made according to the schedule in your Loan Agreement:

  • Payments are due on the specified due dates
  • All payments must be made via M-Pesa PayBill using the provided Business Number and your Loan ID as account number
  • You will receive SMS reminders 3 days before each due date
  • Partial payments are accepted but may not satisfy the full installment obligation
  • If a payment due date falls on a weekend or public holiday, payment is due on the next business day

7. Default & Consequences

You are considered in default if:

  • You fail to make any payment within 7 days of the due date
  • You provide false or misleading information
  • You become bankrupt or insolvent
  • You breach any term of the Loan Agreement

Consequences of Default:

  • Late Fee: KSh 500 or 5% of overdue amount (whichever is higher) after 7 days
  • CRB Listing: Your default will be reported to Credit Reference Bureaus, negatively affecting your credit score
  • Accelerated Payment: The full outstanding balance becomes immediately due
  • Legal Action: We may pursue legal action to recover the debt, and you will be liable for all collection costs, including legal fees
  • Debt Collection: We may engage licensed debt collection agencies

⚠️ CRB Warning: Defaulting on your loan will negatively impact your credit score and may affect your ability to access credit from other lenders in the future.

8. Credit Reference Bureaus (CRB)

As a licensed credit provider, we are required by law to report your loan information to licensed Credit Reference Bureaus:

  • Positive Reporting: Timely repayments help build your credit score
  • Negative Reporting: Late payments and defaults are reported and remain on your record for up to 5 years
  • We will notify you at least 14 days before reporting any negative information
  • You have the right to dispute any information reported to CRBs
  • We partner with Metropol CRB and TransUnion CRB

9. Prepayment

✅ Good News! There is no prepayment penalty at Imara Capital. You may repay your loan in full at any time without additional fees. Early repayment reduces the total interest you pay.

To prepay your loan, contact our customer support or make a payment via M-Pesa PayBill for the full outstanding balance.

10. Loan Disbursement

Once you accept the Loan Agreement, funds will be disbursed as follows:

  • Disbursement is made via M-Pesa STK Push to your registered M-Pesa number
  • Funds typically arrive within 1 hour of acceptance
  • For asset finance, funds may be sent directly to the vendor
  • We are not responsible for delays caused by network issues or incorrect M-Pesa details

11. Cooling Off Period

You have the right to cancel your loan agreement within 3 days of signing ("Cooling Off Period") at no cost. To cancel, contact us immediately at 0700 000 000 or hello@imaracapital.co.ke. If you have already received funds, you must return the full principal amount within the cooling-off period.

12. Debt Collection

If you default, we may use licensed debt collection agencies to recover the debt. Collection practices will comply with Kenyan law and industry best practices:

  • Collection calls will only be made during reasonable hours (8 AM – 8 PM)
  • We will not use harassment, threats, or abusive language
  • We will not contact your employer or family except to verify information or with your consent
  • If you are experiencing financial hardship, contact us to discuss alternative repayment arrangements
